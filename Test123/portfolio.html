About me

Hi, I'm Risa. I am a budding UI/UX designed who recently completed a six-month boot camp in UI/UX design through the University of Minnesota. I also received my bachelor's from the University of Minnesota, majoring in strategic communications though the Hubbard School of Journalism and Mass Communication. My last four roles have been working for startups in the Twin Cities ranging from marketing to events, humans resources and operations. From my work at the smallest of startups to Forbes, and even across the world at a wildlife non-profit in Kenya, I pride myself in being a jack-of-all-trades who is strategic, creative, collaborative and adaptable. In my free time, I enjoy traveling (who doesn't?), being active, fostering dogs and cooking. Read more about my hobbies on this page.
